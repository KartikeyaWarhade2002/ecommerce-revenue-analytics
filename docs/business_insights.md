# Business Insights

## Insight 1 — Highest Revenue Product Category

**Business Question:** Which product category generates the highest revenue?

**Finding:** The **Health & Beauty** category generated **$1,258,681.34** in revenue, making it the highest-performing product category in the dataset.

**Recommendation:** Prioritize inventory planning, targeted marketing campaigns and promotional activities for the Health & Beauty category while analyzing its success factors to improve lower-performing categories.

---

## Insight 2 — Second Highest Revenue Category

**Business Question:** Which product category follows the market leader in revenue?

**Finding:** The **Watches & Gifts** category generated **$1,205,005.68**, making it the second-largest revenue contributor.

**Recommendation:** Consider cross-selling and bundled promotions between Health & Beauty and Watches & Gifts to increase average basket value.

---

## Insight 3 — Highest Revenue Product

**Business Question:** Which individual product generates the highest revenue?

**Finding:** Product **bb50f2e236e5eea0100680137654686c** from the **Health & Beauty** category generated **$63,885.00**, making it the highest revenue-producing product.

## Insight 4 — Highest Value Customer

**Business Question:** Which customer contributes the highest revenue?

**Finding:** Customer **0a0a92112bd4c708ca5fde585afaa872** from **Rio de Janeiro (RJ)** spent **$13,440.00**, making them the highest-value customer.

**Recommendation:** Introduce loyalty programs, personalized offers and premium customer experiences to improve retention among high-value customers.

---

## Insight 5 — Monthly Revenue Performance

**Business Question:** How does revenue change over time?

**Finding:** Revenue increased steadily throughout 2017, peaked in **November 2017 ($1,010,271.37)** and remained strong during the first half of 2018. Very low revenue recorded in September 2016 and September 2018 reflects incomplete reporting periods rather than weak business performance.

**Recommendation:** Analyze the factors driving peak sales periods and replicate successful seasonal campaigns during future high-demand months.

---

## Insight 6 — Average Order Value

**Business Question:** How much does a customer spend per completed order?

**Finding:** The **Average Order Value (AOV)** is **$137.75**, indicating the average revenue generated from each completed order.

**Recommendation:** Increase Average Order Value through product bundles, cross-selling, upselling and minimum-order promotional offers.

---

## Insight 7 — Customer Retention

**Business Question:** How effectively does the business retain customers?

**Finding:** The **Repeat Customer Rate** is **3.05%**, indicating that relatively few customers placed multiple orders during the analysis period.

**Recommendation:** Improve retention through loyalty programs, personalized recommendations, post-purchase engagement and targeted promotional campaigns.

---

## Insight 8 — Payment Method Preferences

**Business Question:** Which payment methods do customers prefer?

**Finding:** **Credit Card** is the dominant payment method with **74,883 orders**, significantly exceeding **Boleto (19,614)**, **Voucher (2,648)** and **Debit Card (1,520)**.

**Recommendation:** Continue optimizing the credit card checkout experience while encouraging adoption of alternative payment methods to reduce dependency on a single payment channel.
