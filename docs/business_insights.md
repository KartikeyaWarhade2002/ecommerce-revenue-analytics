# Business Insights

## Insight 1 — Highest Revenue Category

**Problem:** Which product category generates the most revenue?

**Finding:** The **Health & Beauty** category generated **$1,258,681.34** in revenue, making it the highest-performing category in the portfolio.

**Recommendation:** Prioritize inventory planning, targeted marketing campaigns, and promotional offers for the Health & Beauty category while analyzing the factors driving its success to improve lower-performing categories.

---

## Insight 2 — Second Highest Revenue Category

**Problem:** Which product category follows the market leader in revenue?

**Finding:** The **Watches & Gifts** category generated **$1,205,005.68** in revenue, making it the second-highest revenue contributor and only slightly behind Health & Beauty.

**Recommendation:** Develop cross-selling and bundled promotions between Health & Beauty and Watches & Gifts to increase customer basket value and maximize revenue.

## Insight 5 — Monthly Revenue Trend

**Problem:** How has revenue changed over time?

**Finding:** Revenue increased steadily throughout 2017, reached its highest value in **November 2017 ($1,010,271.37)**, and remained strong during the first half of 2018. The very low revenue recorded in **September 2016 ($267.36)** and **September 2018 ($145.00)** reflects incomplete periods in the dataset rather than typical business performance.

**Recommendation:** Investigate the factors contributing to the strong performance during late 2017 and replicate successful marketing campaigns, seasonal promotions, or inventory strategies during future peak sales periods.

---

## Insight 6 — Average Order Value (AOV)

**Problem:** What is the average revenue generated per order?

**Finding:** The **Average Order Value (AOV)** is **$137.75**, indicating that customers spend approximately this amount on each completed order.

**Recommendation:** Increase AOV through product bundles, cross-selling, upselling, and minimum-order discounts to improve revenue without relying solely on acquiring new customers.

---

## Insight 7 — Repeat Customer Rate

**Problem:** How effectively does the business retain customers?

**Finding:** The **Repeat Customer Rate** is **3.05%**, indicating that only a small proportion of customers placed more than one order during the analysis period.

**Recommendation:** Improve customer retention through loyalty programs, personalized email campaigns, post-purchase engagement, and targeted discount offers to encourage repeat purchases.

---

## Insight 8 — Payment Method Preferences

**Problem:** Which payment methods are most frequently used by customers?

**Finding:** **Credit Card** is the dominant payment method with **74,883 orders**, significantly exceeding **Boleto (19,614 orders)**, **Voucher (2,648 orders)**, and **Debit Card (1,520 orders)**.

**Recommendation:** Continue optimizing the credit card checkout experience while encouraging adoption of alternative payment methods through targeted incentives to reduce dependency on a single payment channel.
